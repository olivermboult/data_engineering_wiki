# Business Context & Success Criteria

## Purpose

Define why the platform exists, which outcomes it must enable, and how success is measured.

## Outcomes

Prioritized use cases, measurable success metrics, and a benefits tracking plan.

## Decisions to Make

- Objectives, scope, and value hypotheses
- Success metrics (KPIs/OKRs) and measurement cadence
- SLAs/SLOs (freshness, availability, latency)
- Adoption goals and training needs
