# Billing & Cost Management

## Purpose

Control and optimize spend with budget guardrails and showback/chargeback.

## Outcomes

Predictable costs, alerts for anomalies, and transparent allocation.

## Decisions to Make

- Capacity model and sizing
- Cost allocation/showback
- Budget limits & alerts
- Off-peak scheduling and optimization

## Standards & Patterns

- Refresh and heavy jobs off-peak
- Optimize storage vs. compute trade-offs
- Cost tagging/metadata

## Deliverables & Artifacts

- Cost model
- Budget guardrails & alerts
- Monthly showback reports

## Checklist

- [ ] Capacity rightsized
- [ ] Budgets & alerts active
- [ ] Cost dashboards published
- [ ] Anomaly playbook defined
