# Reliability, Backup & Disaster Recovery

## Purpose

Ensure recoverability and continuity aligned to RTO/RPO targets.

## Outcomes

Validated backup/restore procedures and disaster recovery runbooks.

## Decisions to Make

- RTO/RPO by asset
- Backup/snapshot approach
- DR regions/capacity and failover paths
- Restore validation cadence

## Standards & Patterns

- Automate snapshots and retention
- Test restores regularly
- Document cross-dependencies (shortcuts, externals)

## Deliverables & Artifacts

- DR plan & playbooks
- Backup/restore SOPs
- Evidence of restore tests

## Checklist

- [ ] Backups configured
- [ ] Restores tested
- [ ] DR plan approved
- [ ] Failover comms prepared
