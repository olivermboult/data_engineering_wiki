# Information Architecture & Scope

## Purpose

Describe the information landscape: subject areas, key entities, definitions, and scope boundaries.

## Outcomes

A subject-area map, conceptual model, and glossary that anchor design and build.

## Decisions to Make

- Subject areas and key entities
- Canonical definitions and business terms
- Reference/master data approach
- Scope and change/versioning policy

## Standards & Patterns

- Maintain a business glossary
- Use conceptual models before logical/physical
- Align entity naming with reporting vocabulary

## Deliverables & Artifacts

- Subject-area map
- Conceptual & logical models (high level)
- Glossary/business dictionary

## Checklist

- [ ] Subject areas defined
- [ ] Key entities listed
- [ ] Glossary published
- [ ] Scope boundaries agreed
