# Change Management, Delivery & Risk

## Purpose

Deliver safely and iteratively with visibility of risks, dependencies, and cutovers.

## Outcomes

A delivery roadmap, RAID log, and controlled release cadence.

## Decisions to Make

- Release cadence & change windows
- Design authority/steering forums
- Dependency and vendor coordination
- Parallel run and cutover plans

## Standards & Patterns

- Lightweight change control for low-risk items
- Formal approvals for high-risk changes
- Regular RAID reviews

## Deliverables & Artifacts

- Delivery plan & roadmap
- RAID/dependency logs
- Cutover/runbooks & comms

## Checklist

- [ ] Roadmap approved
- [ ] RAID active and reviewed
- [ ] Cutover rehearsed
- [ ] Comms templates ready
