# Metadata, Catalog & Lineage

## Purpose

Improve discoverability and trust through rich metadata, lineage, and certification.

## Outcomes

A searchable catalog with ownership, definitions, and end-to-end lineage.

## Decisions to Make

- Catalog platform and scope
- Ownership & certification workflows
- Lineage capture and retention
- Metadata completeness standards

## Standards & Patterns

- Mandatory tags: owner, SLA, sensitivity
- Automated lineage where possible
- Certify/endorse content based on criteria

## Deliverables & Artifacts

- Metadata standards
- Lineage diagrams
- Certification/endorsement workflow

## Checklist

- [ ] Assets registered in catalog
- [ ] Ownership & tags complete
- [ ] Lineage available
- [ ] Certification criteria applied
