# Support & Operations (Run)

## Purpose

Define how the platform is supported day-to-day with clear SLAs and playbooks.

## Outcomes

Operational readiness, on-call processes, and continuous improvement loops.

## Decisions to Make

- Support hours & SLAs
- Severity definitions & escalation
- Ticketing workflows & response targets
- Problem management & RCA

## Standards & Patterns

- Triage and incident templates
- Blameless post-mortems
- Ops calendar and change freeze windows

## Deliverables & Artifacts

- Support SLAs
- Incident playbooks
- Ops calendar
- PIR template

## Checklist

- [ ] SLAs agreed
- [ ] On-call rota in place
- [ ] Incident runbooks linked
- [ ] PIRs tracked
