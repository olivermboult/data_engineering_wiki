# Monitoring & Observability

## Purpose

Provide end-to-end visibility into health, performance, and costs with timely alerts.

## Outcomes

Operational dashboards, alert routing, and SLO-driven monitoring.

## Decisions to Make

- Metrics & logs to capture
- Alert channels and severity
- SLOs/SLIs and reporting cadence
- Central logging schema and retention

## Standards & Patterns

- Unified monitoring hub
- Alert noise reduction and deduplication
- Cost anomaly detection policies

## Deliverables & Artifacts

- Monitoring architecture
- Alert rules & routing
- Operational dashboards

## Checklist

- [ ] Metrics captured for key components
- [ ] Alerts actionable and routed
- [ ] SLOs tracked
- [ ] Runbooks linked from alerts
