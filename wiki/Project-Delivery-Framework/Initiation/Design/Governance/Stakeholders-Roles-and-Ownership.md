# Stakeholders, Roles & Ownership

## Purpose

Ensure clear ownership, decision rights, and engagement for all platform assets and activities.

## Outcomes

A RACI and engagement plan covering sources, models, reports, and operations.

## Decisions to Make

- Sponsors, owners, stewards, engineers, report creators, consumers
- Decision cadence (steering/design reviews)
- Escalation paths and approvals

## Standards & Patterns

- Map ownership to security groups
- Keep a single RACI per asset type
- Define break-glass and emergency procedures

## Deliverables & Artifacts

- Stakeholder map
- RACI matrices
- Engagement calendar and forums

## Checklist

- [ ] Owners identified for each asset type
- [ ] RACI published
- [ ] Decision forums scheduled
- [ ] Escalation paths documented
