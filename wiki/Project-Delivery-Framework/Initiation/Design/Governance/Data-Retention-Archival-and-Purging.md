# Data Retention, Archival & Purging

## Purpose

Manage lifecycle of data to meet compliance while optimizing cost and performance.

## Outcomes

Retention matrices, archival tiers, and auditable purge processes.

## Decisions to Make

- Retention by classification/asset
- Archival tiers & formats
- Purge/tombstoning and GDPR deletion evidence
- Legal holds

## Standards & Patterns

- Align retention to value & regulation
- Balance Delta time travel with storage cost
- Maintain deletion audit trail

## Deliverables & Artifacts

- Retention matrix
- Archival design
- Purge runbooks & audit logs

## Checklist

- [ ] Retention defined
- [ ] Archival tested
- [ ] Purge procedures documented
- [ ] Legal hold process verified
